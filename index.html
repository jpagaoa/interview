Interview

Pursuing in Graphic Design.

November 15, 2019

John Pagaoa

Why do you want to be a designer?

Since I was a kid a love to draw, paint, and photography. I always fascinated to learn new things that involve designing. I worked for different companies (warehouses, factories, hotels etc...) and I didn't see myself working in any other field.

What makes you nervous about becoming a designer?

I am nervous because it is a very competitive job. I am worried if I graduated I can't able to find a job.

What excites you about design?

When I finally find a job in a field that I would really enjoy to work and maybe more income.

What are some areas of opportunity to work on?

I noticed that I am more interested in typography and redesign the brand. But anything that graphic design job I would be happy.

What aspects of graphic design to you gravitate towards?

Critical thinking, very artistic, have many experienced in fast pace environment and problem solver. In addition, I like to challenge myself anything that comes along my way.

What are the roles and responsibilities of a graphic design in society?

For my knowledge it is an important graphic design in society because we are moving forward about our technologies and in this era we are connected through devices all the time. As a graphic design it is important to explore and create new images, ideas and design as part of our fast growing technologies.

When/where/why are you at your most creative/productive?

Mostly in the morning when I have a good night's sleep. Also in a quiet place with a good internet connection.

What are your comfort levels with giving and receiving feedback? How might you improve your skills here? What strategies have worked for you?

I like receiving feedback after critiquing my project/assignment with my professor or fellow students. very open to suggestion and willing to learn new ideas as much as possible before I graduate.

What is a website?

website is to show your creative skills and many other useful resources. It is a part of our lifestyle in this era. we now do many things online for instance shopping, groceries, ordering food so on and so forth. website is where you published your work online so other people can see. whether business or personal information.
